HOW TO GET PRIMOGEMS BY DOING IN-GAME THINGS

  • Complete Quests and Missions
    • Main Story Quests: Progress through the main Archon Quests and Story Quests. These often reward Primogems upon completion.
    • Commission Quests: Daily commissions give you a small amount of Primogems every day. Don’t forget to complete them daily!
  • Explore the World
    • Unlocking Statues of The Seven: Each time you unlock or level up a Statue of The Seven, you get Primogems.
    • Find and Complete World Quests: These are scattered throughout Teyvat and can provide rewards including Primogems.
  • Complete Achievements
    • Check out the Achievement menu in the game for various tasks that, once completed, reward you with Primogems.
  • Participate in Events
    • Limited-Time Events: Events often provide opportunities to earn Primogems. Keep an eye on official announcements and in-game notices.
  • Open Chests
    • Exploration Rewards: Many chests around Teyvat contain Primogems. The rarer the chest, the more Primogems you might find!
  • Use the Adventurer’s Handbook
    • Challenges: Completing certain challenges in the Adventurer’s Handbook rewards you with Primogems.
  • Complete Domains and Bosses
    • Domains: Some domains offer Primogems as rewards.
    • Weekly Bosses: Defeating weekly bosses like Stormterror or Andrius grants you Primogems.
  • Participate in Spiral Abyss
    • Floor Rewards: Clearing floors in the Spiral Abyss, especially higher ones, can reward you with Primogems.
  • Refer Friends
    • If you’re part of a referral program or special promotions, inviting friends to play can sometimes yield rewards.
  • Stay Updated with Official Sources
    • Patch Notes and Updates: Occasionally, miHoYo (now HoYoverse) releases codes or special promotions through their official social media channels or during live streams.

    • Remember, always avoid any third-party sites or services that claim to provide free Primogems, as they could be scams or lead to account security issues. Stick to the methods outlined above to safely and legitimately increase your Primogem stash. Happy adventuring!
